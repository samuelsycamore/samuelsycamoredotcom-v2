Hi there! Sam Sycamore here.

I live in the Santa Cruz Mountains and work as a web developer and writer. I'm the founder of Sycamore Design.

From 2016-2020 I hosted The Good Life Revival Podcast.

In 2019 I released Foraging North America: The Botany, Taxonomy, and Ecology of Edible Wild Plants.

In 2018 I published Introduction to Foraging: A Beginner's Guide to Gathering Wild Foods With Confidence.

From 2012-2014 I freelanced as a music journalist. Here's a piece I wrote on the local music scene in my hometown of Louisville, KY for Impose Magazine in 2014.
